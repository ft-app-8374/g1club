## Design Implications

1. **Not strictly Group 1** — admin must curate which races go in each round
2. **Mixed grades** — UI should show grade badge (G1, G2, G3, Listed, SC)
3. **Multi-venue rounds** — single round can span Sydney + Melbourne on the same day
4. **Multi-day rounds** — Round 6 and Round 12 span multiple days but share one cutoff
5. **Variable round sizes** — from 1 race to 5 races per round
6. **Cutoff logic** — "1hr before first race on the card" means earliest race across all venues in the round
